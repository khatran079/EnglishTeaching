About Me

My name is Kha Tran, the founder of the Ant English. I did my bachelor degree in Australia and with various course certifications. My goal is to reach out numbers of Vietnamese students, adults who are eager to learn English to improve their language skills from general to academic.

From my personal experiences of learning and teaching English, I do understand how hard it is to use English fluently. It is combination of hard studying and persistence to reach the final goal is to be confident in using English.

With me, I can help you to archieve your goals and dreams that using English as a tool to study, communicate with people around the world while you are traveling or for busines purposes.
